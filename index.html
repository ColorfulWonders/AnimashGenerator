11/1/2019

I offically launched this generator, yippee!!!<.p>

Before I say anything else I just wanna give a huge shoutout to Rushapart for letting me use their old animash pairing generator as a base! I can't thank them enough!

Anyway, I made this generator to include more characters and options, so people can make more creative animashes with lesser used pairings and characters! This isn't only for romantic pairings, but can be used for pretty much any dynamic you want!

Credit is appricated but not necessary! Either way I'd love to see what you create! ðŸ’•

Changes from origanal:

  • Added options for selecting a characters role (wether they are a main, side or background character in thier respective movies), since this often dictates how much footage they have/how easy they are to edit with/etc.
  • Added options for selecting a characters species, since some people prefer to ship dogs with dogs, cats with cats, etc.
  • Added more, lesser used characters from movies commonly used in animash. (I'm not gonna list them all but for example: jock and trusty from LATT or FLower from Bambi)
  • Added changelog, the thing you're reading right now!!! :D
